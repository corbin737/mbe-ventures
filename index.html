About Us

Our company is committed to the success of others, and was founded on the principle of offering experienced people to work with organizations to achieve this end. We bring extra support when it is needed for both the governance and delivery of project and organizational strategies.

We customize our services and staffing to meet client requirements and expectations, and bring the extra horsepower when and where needed.

MBE was established in 2007 and operates throughout western Canada. David McElhanney is the principal and founder of the company, providing knowledge, experience and judgement gained during 25 years in business and engineering consulting.
